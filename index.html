Research Areas

Political Status & Negotiations

Analysis of political status, MINURSO negotiations, regional dynamics, and economic development opportunities in Western Sahara.

Foreign Presences & Integration

Mapping foreign military and economic presences, regional integration efforts, and migration flows across Maghreb-Atlantic axis.

Terrorism & Governance

Security threats analysis, governance failures, climate impacts, and resource conflicts driving Sahel instability.

Future Scenarios

Strategic foresight modeling, opportunity windows identification, and policy recommendations for decision-makers.
